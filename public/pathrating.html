How do you like the path animations?

How to use

In order to enable our model to learn which choice of animations looks good or bad, we need to provide training examples of good and bad choices.
Therefore, please rate each of the 3 second animations you see using the buttons above.
The model first chooses which of the elements should be animated and in the second step how to animate them. Thus, please rate the animation of each element independently.
Before you start, enter your name or alias to compete with others. You can check your score on the "Highscores" page.

How to interpret the labels

VERY BAD: The element is animated in an ugly or inappropriate way

BAD: Bad animation but it goes in the right direction

OKAY: Simple but good animation, e.g. comparable to PowerPoint animations

GOOD: Cool animation but could still be improved

VERY GOOD: Perfect animation, could be used in production
